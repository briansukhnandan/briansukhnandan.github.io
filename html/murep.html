Some starter kits to check out:



The Elegoo Starter Kit features all of the equipment that we have used in class, and even more. This is also the same kit I had started out with when I started learning this stuff.






The SainSmart Starter Kit is a cheaper alternative to the Elegoo kit. It doesn't have that many things to work with, however all of the lessons we did in class can be recreated.





The Elegoo Mega Kit is much more expensive than the others but contains tons of stuff to keep you occupied for a long time, and also has many projects for you to do.
